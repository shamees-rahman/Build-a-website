Chembra Peak
Chembra Peak trek is unquestionably one of the most picturesque and most exquisite treks in all of Kerala, if not the entire south. A heart-shaped lake, that is believed to have never been dried, dwells on the way to the top. With majestic mountains standing tall amidst varying hues of lush green, upon which foamy white clouds breathe, Chembra Peak definitely is a visual treat fit for any traveller's dream.
